Standing Seam Metal Roofing

Durable, sleek, weather-tight roofing system designed for longevity.

Standing seam metal roofs use interlocking panels that run vertically from ridge to eave, hiding all fasteners for a clean, modern look. They’re incredibly durable, weather-tight, and built to handle heavy snow, ice, and wind for decades with minimal maintenance.

FAQs

How long does installation take?

Most standing seam metal roofs are installed in 3–5 days, depending on roof size, slope, and trim complexity. Smaller or simpler roofs can be completed even faster.

Can you repair an existing metal roof?

Yes. We specialize in repairing leaks, seams, and trim on existing metal roofs, extending their life and maintaining a clean, professional appearance.

How does a metal roof handle snow and ice?

Metal roofs naturally shed snow and ice, preventing buildup and excess weight. We also install snow guards to control the slide and improve safety around your home or building.

What’s included in the quote?

Your quote includes materials, panels, trim, flashing, snow guards if requested, and full installation. We provide a detailed, itemized estimate with no hidden costs.

How soon can you start my project?

Typical lead times are 2–4 weeks during peak season. After your site visit, we’ll confirm a schedule that works best for your timeline and weather conditions.
