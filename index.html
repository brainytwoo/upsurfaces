FAQs

How long does installation take?

Most projects are completed in 1–2 days once the base is ready, though larger areas or complex layouts may take longer.

Do you replace old surfaces or work over them?

We can repair and refinish existing concrete or asphalt, or build new paver installations depending on your site’s condition.

How do your surfaces hold up in winter?

All of our work is designed for freeze–thaw cycles and snow removal, using materials that perform well in northern climates.

What’s included in the quote?

Every quote is itemized and firm for 30 days, covering site prep, materials, and labor — no hidden costs.

How soon can you start my project?
